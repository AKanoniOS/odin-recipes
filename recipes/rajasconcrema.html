Rajas Con Crema

This wonderful Mexican dish is a blend of Pablano Pepper, Corn, and Cream. It serves well over a tortilla. It is filling and a great comfort meal.

Ingredients

  • Corn
  • Pablano Peppers
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Cream Cheese

Instructions

Put the butter, milk, and cream cheese together in a sauce pan and heat while whisking together over a medium heat. Saut√© the pablano peppers with some olive oil or butter. Cook the corn and add the peppers and corn into the crema.
